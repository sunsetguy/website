ROBERT BOSITO

With 18 years of experience working as a photographer’s assistant, Robert Bosito has gone through all types of shoots in any situation imaginable. In fact, most know Robert as Obet, G-nie’s loyal and trusty assistant. After much encouragement from G-nie and the rest of the Adphoto team, he is now a full pledged photographer. He has mastered the technicalities of both food and product photography with an expanding portfolio on talent, architectural and special effects. But his humble beginnings fuels his passion in sharing with new assistants and photographers of Adphoto the technical expertise as well as the life lessons he’s gathered in his years of experience with Adphoto. This makes him truly invaluable, and a remarkable master photographer in the making.
