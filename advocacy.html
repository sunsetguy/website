ADPHOTO, INC founder and commercial photographer John K. Chua began Photography with a Difference (PWD) in 2008. To date, it has involved more than a thousand photographers in the country in helping hundreds of persons with different disabilities. For his work, John received an Apolinario Mabini Rehabilitation Volunteer award in 2011.

In addition, he has worked, since 1970 with a Northern Luzon ethnic tribes to produce anthropological documentation of their landscape, people and rituals, and has brought together volunteers from across the country in initiatives to restore the Banaue Rice Terraces (a UNESCO World Heritage Site).

Currently, he works hand in hand with the Armed Forces of the Philippines on training it combat photographers on creating more beautiful images.

He is also known as a volunteer zookeeper for Maali, Manila Zoo's lone elephant.
