G-NIE

G-nie found her love of photography while art-directing shoots as an Adphoto client in 1992. After she joined Adphoto, John mentored her and helped her become the country’s most awarded advertising photographer. She is currently the only Filipino member of the elite Master Photographers Association (MPA) based in the UK. Her high-speed special effects shots of Boysen Paints earned a number of accolades from international advertising award-giving organizations, including Spikes and Cannes Lion.
